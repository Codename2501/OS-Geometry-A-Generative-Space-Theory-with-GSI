# Chapter 4 — Stability of GSI

## 1. Classification of Stability
The growth of GSI reveals  
the stability of the generative system.

---

### ● 1. Convergent (Stable)

$$
\int_{\text{GSI}} f(x)\, d_{\text{OS}}x < \infty
$$

- Trajectory returns toward a center  
- $d_{\text{OS}}x$ becomes small  

---

### ● 2. Divergent (Unstable)

$$
\int_{\text{GSI}} f(x)\, d_{\text{OS}}x \to \infty
$$

- Trajectory expands outward  
- $d_{\text{OS}}x$ grows  

---

### ● 3. Chaotic (Boundary)
- GSI increases and decreases  
- Stability determined statistically  

---

## 2. Stability is Determined by G
The nature of the generative function $G$  
determines the stability of GSI.
